Plants & Animals

Cuc Phuong National Park

Plants

Lotus Tinh De

Sen Tinh De is very rare, considered the leader of all kinds of flowers, elegant, pure, favored by kings and writers in ancient times and planted many varieties in Kinh Bac, Kinh Do. Perhaps, also because of its special shape and rarity, Tinh De Lien is considered a good omen, bringing prosperity and prosperity.The Pure Land Lotus never blooms individually, on the same flower base there are always two flowers. These two flowers are equally large, have the same color and bloom together. The color of the Tinh De lotus is not much different from that of an ordinary lotus, only the buds and petals are somewhat smaller and more delicate. The Tinh De Lotus fragrance is also more intense than the normal lotus scent. When used to make tea or make water, the flavor will last longer. And even though the fragrance is flowing, the Tinh De lotus has the power to fascinate the human heart with a gentle feeling, not causing people to feel dark and headache.

Animals

Stone turtle

Stone Turtle (also known as Pond Turtle) is a type of freshwater turtle in the Emydidae and Bataguridae families. The most prominent species of Stone Turtle in the world include the European Stone Turtle, the Western Stone Turtle (America), the Asian Giant Stone Turtle, the Chinese Stone Turtle, and the Japanese Stone Turtle. There is also Vietnam Stone Turtle with another name is Annam Stone Turtle.

Pygathrix nemaeus

Pygathrix nemaeus is one of the most beautiful primates in the world. They are found in Laos, Vietnam, and Cambodia and are in high danger of extinction. This species has a slim body, multi-colored hair, black forehead, thick facial hair forming a face plate, gray-white to gray. Their necks and chests have a reddish color that ranges from bright to pale. They usually eat fruit, forest leaves, corn, potatoes, cassava, green vegetables.

Melogale cucphuongensis

German scientists have discovered and announced a new species of ferret-cheeked ferret badger scientific name is Melogale cucphuongensis. This is a research result for many years and is published in the international specialized journal Der Zoologische Garten. The new species has a skull shape and a number of different features compared to the weasel species have been scientifically documented. Some identifiable features of the Melogale cucphuongensis are the dark brown head and body, white spot on the forehead, the long mustache, from neck to shoulder is a white stripe with black embossing. turn on. Belly hair is much lighter in color than the back. The results of DNA analysis show that the species of the ferret-cheeked ferret badger is quite different from the species Melogale personata, Melogale moschata (ferret-cheeked ferret badger), has a wide distribution in Vietnam and two other species Melogale orientalis and Melogale everetti are distributed in Asia.

Visitor Information Centers

The visitor center is built with the purpose of providing information on the value of natural resources and environment in Cuc Phuong to raise conservation awareness for tourists as well as local communities living around around the Garden. With the pictures shown, the information provided at the Center, visitors will partly understand about Cuc Phuong in particular and the great values of nature in general, and then when visiting the forest of love Nature in each of us will be multiplied, the more we value it.

The best time to explore

September through November is the best time to visit Jiuzhaigou. October 15 through 30 is the golden time for viewing colorful leaves. In fact, different seasons give Jiuzhaigou different kind of beauty. It all depends on your budget, tolerance for large crowds, your personal interests and, another time of year may suit you better.
